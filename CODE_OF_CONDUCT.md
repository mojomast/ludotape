# Code of Conduct

We pledge a harassment-free, welcoming project regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Be respectful, constructive, and accountable. Do not harass, threaten, demean, publish private information, or disrupt collaboration. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently ban participants for unacceptable conduct.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the channel from which you received the project. Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept as confidential as practical.
